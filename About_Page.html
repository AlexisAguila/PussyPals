How We Started

We came up for the idea of a cat match-making website when one rainy afternoon one of our group members saw a lonely cat sitting on the porch of its owners house clearly distressed about his/her failure to find a compatible mate. Then BOOM! The idea of Pussy Pals was born! With access to a stable internet connection and a human owner with opposable thumbs any feline can find a friend! Is your cat fat and out of shape? Let Pussy Pals come to the rescue! With a friend to hangout, catch mice, or run away from the neighbors dog your cat will surely get into shape in no time! Join the website today with our growing site of users!! The paw-siblities are endless!
